This is an audio game. A headset is essential!

Start your game by listening to the Outer World adventure.

As you progress through your Outer World, your Inner World is gradually unlocked and always available.

At the end of each audio segment you may be given a choice. Tap your preference to proceed.

To Fast Forward: Move your finger around the outer circle that surrounds the Play button.

Each time you make a decision, you have a brief opportunity to rewind and choose the other option.

At the end of THE GAME you may return to the beginning and listen to the unique audiobook you created!
